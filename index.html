About Me

Always Deliver More Than Expected - Larry Page

I am currently a Data Scientist in the recommendation system team at one of the unicorn companies. My main responsibility is building large-scale recommendation systems for the marketplace with billions of products and millions of monthly transactions. Apart from that, I am also responsible for developing cross-selling models and ranking systems as well as an endless list of customer segmentations based on multiple different features. On a daily basis, I performed exploratory data analysis, impact analysis, and lists of ad-hoc tasks related to data science from multiple stakeholders such as Product Manager, Software Architect, Data Science Manager, and VPs. In order to support my works, I spend a few working days every month reading recent papers about machine learning and recommendation system. I spend most of my spare time during the weekends as a contributor at Medium and to upskill myself.

A bit of flashback, I graduated from Master of Data Science at Monash University, Australia in December 2019. I had been recognized as the only student who graduated with a full GPA. During the 2 years of master study, I have achieved 10+ academic awards at Monash University including awards as the only student who achieves perfect marks for 2 units in the Master of Data Science Course history. Outside the formal academic routine, I have participated in data science-related competitions and managed to win 2 competitions namely Monash Datathon sponsored by National Australia Bank and FIT Kaggle Competition.

In the last 2 years, I have been involved in many data science-related projects and researches, with topics ranging from text similarity, social media analytics, machine learning, data analytics to visualization. I also spent my time volunteering as a Python Programming Tutor for the new graduate students at Monash University for 2 years.

Skills : Recommendation System, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Social Media Analytics, Data Wrangling, Data Science, Data Analytics, Statistics, Data Visualization, ETL, Topic Modelling, Text Similarity, Sentiment Analysis, Recommender System, Data Analytics
Tools & Languages : Python, SQL, R, Keras, Pyspark, Pytorch, Ms. Power BI, Tableau, Plotly, R-Shiny, Github, Google Cloud Platform, Bigquery, Redash, BigqueryML, Dashboard Python, R, Pyspark, Pytorch, SQL, Tableau, Ms.Power BI, R-Shiny, Redash, Bigquery, BigqueryML, Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
